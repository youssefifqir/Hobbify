# Hobbify Design Brief

## Vision
Hobbify is a "friendly mentor" in your pocket (or on your desktop), designed to lower the barrier to entry for new hobbies. It transforms vague interests into actionable, step-by-step journeys.

## Visual Direction
- **Tone:** Warm, encouraging, low-pressure, and approachable.
- **Style:** Clean card-based layout, generous whitespace, soft rounded corners, and a pastel-leaning but vibrant color palette.
- **Imagery:** Friendly illustrations or clear icons for hobby categories (e.g., Cooking, Woodworking, Gardening).
- **Inspiration:** Drawing from the clean, community-focused mobile UI provided, adapted for a responsive desktop experience.

## Core Screens
1. **Discovery (Home):** A library of hobbies with search and robust filtering (cost, space, time commitment).
2. **Hobby Detail:** Overview of a specific hobby—what to expect, total time, and the "start journey" trigger.
3. **Stage-by-Stage Guide:** The meat of the app. A guided path (e.g., "Week 1: Setup") with checkable steps and a prominent progress bar.
4. **Dashboard:** A simple view for users to see their "In Progress" hobbies and achievements.
5. **Auth (Sign Up/Login):** Minimalist and welcoming entry points.

## Content Shape
- **Hobby Cards:** Name, Category, Cost Tier ($, $$, $$$), Space Needed (Small, Medium, Large), Time Commitment, Difficulty.
- **Guides:** Categorized into stages, each containing individual actionable steps.
- **Status:** Only "published," curated content is visible to the consumer.
