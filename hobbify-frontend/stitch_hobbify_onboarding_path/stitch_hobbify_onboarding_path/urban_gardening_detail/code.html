About the hobby

Urban gardening is the soulful practice of cultivating plants in an urban environment. Whether you have a tiny balcony, a sunny windowsill, or just a spare corner in your living room, you can transform your space into a green sanctuary. It’s a low-pressure way to reconnect with nature, improve your air quality, and even grow your own fresh herbs for cooking.
