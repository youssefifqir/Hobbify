Material Matters

Terracotta is breathable but dries fast. Plastic holds moisture but may overheat in direct sun.

Size it Right

Ensure your pot is at least 2 inches wider than the root ball of your plant to allow for growth.

lightbulb Pro Tip

Always check for drainage holes at the bottom. If they're missing, you'll need a masonry bit to drill them yourself!
